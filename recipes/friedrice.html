Lasagna

Description:

Fried rice is a popular and versatile dish enjoyed across many cultures, especially in Asian cuisine. It's made by stir-frying cooked rice with a mix of vegetables, sauces, and often eggs or meat. Originally a way to use up leftover rice, it has evolved into a flavorful, standalone meal that's quick and easy to prepare. The combination of soy sauce, garlic, and a hot pan gives it a signature savory taste.

You can make fried rice as simple or as loaded as you like. Add eggs for protein, toss in leftover veggies, or even stir in chicken, shrimp, or tofu. It’s perfect for a fast lunch, dinner, or a satisfying midnight snack. With a few pantry staples and some cold rice, you can whip up this dish in under 15 minutes.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups cooked rice (preferably cold, leftover rice)
  • 2 tablespoons oil (vegetable or sesame)
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup chopped vegetables (like carrots, peas, capsicum, onion)
  • 2 eggs (optional)
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Spring onions (for garnish - optional)

Steps:

  1. Heat oil in a pan or wok.
  2. Add garlic and sauté for a few seconds.
  3. Add chopped vegetables and stir-fry until tender.
  4. Push veggies aside, scramble eggs in the same pan (if using).
  5. Add cooked rice and mix everything well.
  6. Pour in soy sauce, add salt and pepper, stir-fry for 2-3 minutes.
  7. Garnish with spring onions and serve hot.
