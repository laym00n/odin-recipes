Lasagna

Description:

Maggi is a beloved instant noodle brand, especially popular in India and across Asia. Known for its quick cooking time and signature masala flavor, Maggi has become a comfort food staple in many households. Whether you're a student looking for a fast meal or someone craving a nostalgic bite, Maggi is the go-to choice. It's tasty, affordable, and can be made in under 10 minutes.

Over the years, Maggi has evolved beyond just a snackâ€”people now customize it with vegetables, cheese, eggs, or even meat to make it more filling. Its versatility makes it a great base for quick meal experiments. Whether plain or loaded, Maggi remains a flavorful and satisfying option when you want something hot, simple, and delicious.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pack of Maggi instant noodles
  • 1 and 1/2 cups of water
  • Maggi tastemaker spice mix (comes inside the pack)
  • 1/2 onion, finely chopped
  • 1 small tomato, chopped
  • 1/4 cup green peas or chopped carrots
  • A pinch of red chili flakes or black pepper
  • 1 egg or grated cheese
  • teaspoon butter or ghee (for richness)

Steps:

  1. Boil 1 and 1/2 cups of water in a pan.
  2. Add the Maggi noodles block to the boiling water.
  3. Sprinkle in the tastemaker (spice) packet.
  4. Stir and cook on medium heat.
  5. Add optional veggies or cheese if desired.
  6. Cook until water is mostly absorbed and noodles are soft (about 2-3 minutes).
  7. Serve hot and enjoy immediately.
